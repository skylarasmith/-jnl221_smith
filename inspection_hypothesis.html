Data Hypothesis: Chain restaurants in Syracuse had more non-critical violations than independently owned buisnesses during 2025.

I think this is a good data hypothesis because I can use the information provided in the datasetregarding the number of restaurants that had non-critical violations to either prove or disprove my claim. This hypothesis raises a question of whether local buisnesses with a smaller staff are paying more attention to meeting regulated expectations more often than bigger corporations; especially post pandemic, when small buisnesses took a hit, I am interested to see the difference in how each establishment performed.
